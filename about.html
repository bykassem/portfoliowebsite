About

As a lead product designer with over 10 years of experience building digital products, I am passionate about efficient and intuitive user-centred design in all its forms. My expertise, however, is in digital product design. With well-rounded skills and experience running projects independently or in teams from end to end, I have delivered everything from product strategy, user personas, interviews, user flows, high-end wireframes (UX), interface designs (UI), branding, prototypes, design documentation and development.

As a team leader, I practise integrity, open communication and careful listening to foster positive feedback and team growth. I have extensive experience in a leadership position as co-founder of my own design agency for 7 years. During this time, and with over 100 clients, I became expert at untangling complex ideas to get to the root of stakeholder wants and needs and expressing abstract concepts in a tangible and engaging way.

With a deep understanding of visual communication and development concepts, and a foundation in animation and illustration, I carefully craft more meaningful user interactions through graphic/design narratives. I pride myself on finding the sweet spot between aesthetics and usability.
