About

Interest - As an avid lover of UX, I am always looking for more intuitive and user-centric design solutions to all facets of my life. But particularly my attention is pointed towards our next leap in experiences and the new design solutions that will accompany them. From AR and VR, to Self Driving, Fintech and AI. Each of these industries will be seeing incredible transformation in the next decade as we leave traditional screens and integrate with our spatial environments.

Experience - I have over 10 years of experience building digital products for hundreds of different startups and larger institutions, with well-rounded skills in strategy, design and development. I have experience running projects from end-to-end, delivering everything from design ops, product strategies, UX, UI, branding, prototypes, design systems, and development. I have become expert at untangling complex systems from administrative mortgage applications, to stock market apps, to teaching tools, to community platforms. Each time, untangling intertwined information and abstract ideas into a meaningful human-centric interface and experience.

Leadership - I have accumulated extensive experience in leadership positions as Creative Director and Co-Founder of Half Hunter Design Agency for 7 years, where I worked directly with stakeholders to uncover their needs and present tangible and actionable solutions. During the course of my career I have managed and mentored information architects, designers, copywriters and developers. I believe in practising integrity, open communication and careful listening to foster positive feedback and team growth. I’m also a big proponent of Community with a capital C. Community is at the source of most of my interpersonal engagement, which led me to co-found Good Gorilla, a much-loved coworking space for creatives to aggregate and support each other. At Good Gorilla I organized and led numerous design and AR discussions and workshops with the goal of fostering more collaboration and creativity.

Background - In addition, I have a background in animation and illustration, which supports me in carefully crafting more meaningful user interactions through visual narratives and storytelling techniques.
